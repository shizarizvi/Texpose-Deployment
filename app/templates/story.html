Our Story

How Texpose Began

Texpose was born from a passion for artificial intelligence and a mission to distinguish human creativity from AI-generated content. Our journey started as a final-year project, evolving into a powerful detection tool.

With a deep learning-powered approach, Texpose is here to ensure authenticity in the digital age. One project. One vision. Limitless possibilities.

Shaping the Future

Where We're Headed

We envision a world where AI and human creativity coexist transparently. Our tool empowers educators, writers, and content creators to maintain originality in their work.
